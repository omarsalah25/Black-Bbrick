Our Projects

Ain El Sokhna
Black Brick's Ain El Sokhna projects offer a unique escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Our properties are located along the Red Sea coast, providing breathtaking views of the turquoise waters and pristine beaches. With its luxurious amenities and world-class facilities, Ain El Sokhna is the perfect destination for relaxation and rejuvenation.
New Administrative Capital
Black Brick is at the forefront of development in Egypt's New Administrative Capital, a cutting-edge metropolis designed to meet the needs of the future. Our projects in the NAC offer a blend of modern living and business opportunities, making it the ideal place to live, work, and thrive.
October
Black Brick's October projects are situated in the heart of Cairo's thriving business district. Our properties offer convenient access to major transportation hubs, corporate offices, and a variety of amenities, making them ideal for professionals and families alike.
North Coast
Black Brick's North Coast projects are nestled along the Mediterranean coast, offering a serene escape with the sound of waves crashing against the shore. Our properties feature spacious living areas, modern amenities, and stunning views of the sea, making them the perfect place to unwind and enjoy the beauty of nature.
Fifth Settlement
Black Brick's Fifth Settlement projects are located in one of Cairo's most prestigious neighborhoods. Our properties offer a luxurious lifestyle with access to world-class amenities, including fine dining, shopping, and entertainment venues.
